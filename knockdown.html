Knockdown & Rebuild

At New Dawn Design & Construct, we help homeowners unlock the full potential of their block through smart, design-led knockdown and rebuild solutions. Whether your existing home no longer suits your lifestyle or your site deserves a fresh start, our integrated process makes rebuilding simple, efficient, and rewarding.

Our Canberra-based team manages every step—from demolition permits to final handover—ensuring your new home is tailored to your vision, compliant with local regulations, and built to last. By combining architectural design, approvals, and construction under one roof, we eliminate the guesswork and deliver a home that’s more functional, more beautiful, and future-ready.
